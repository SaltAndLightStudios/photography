Our Story

My husband and I founded Salt and Light Studios with a mission to share the beauty of God's creation through the art of photography. Inspired by Matthew 5:13-16, we strive to be both salt and light in the world of visual storytelling.

I bring years of experience and a unique perspective to every shoot. I believe that every moment has a story, and I am dedicated to telling that story through my lenses.

Whether it's the majesty of nature, the joy of travel, or the quiet moments of everyday life, I approach each project with reverence, creativity, and technical excellence.
