Our Story

My husband and I started Salt and Light Studios with a simple mission: to share the beauty of God’s creation through our art and creativity. Inspired by Matthew 5:13–16, we aim to be a light for the Gospel and honor Christ through the gifts He’s given us.

I’m the photographer behind the lens, but this work is very much a team effort. My husband and I often shoot together, and he supports me in every way possible, from helping carry gear to being a second set of eyes and creative input. We love working side by side and using our talents to honor the Lord and bless the people we meet through our work.

We genuinely love to travel, connect with others, and capture moments that reflect the beauty of God’s creation and the stories unfolding within it. Photography has become a way for us to slow down, be present, serve others, and share hope and light with the people and places we encounter.
