Our Story

My husband and I started Salt and Light Studios with a simple mission: to share the beauty of God’s creation through creativity. Inspired by Matthew 5:13–16, we aim to be a source of light in the way we tell visual stories.

I’m the photographer behind the lens, but this work is very much a team effort. My husband and I often shoot together, and he supports me in every way possible, from helping carry gear to being a second set of eyes and creative input. We love working side by side and using our talents to honor the Lord and bless the people we meet through our work.

We are passionate about travel, connecting with others, and capturing moments that reflect both the beauty of creation around us and the stories within it. Photography, for us, is a way to serve, encourage, and share light with the world around us.

Whether it’s the majesty of nature, the energy of travel, or the quiet moments of everyday life, I approach each shoot with care, creativity, and attention to detail. It’s more than just an image, it’s a reflection of something meaningful.
